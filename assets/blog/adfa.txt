Work and stress should not have an ounce of space in the mind. Life is too short for that. Enjoy every moment. Often, these feel like empty words, but for some reason, there are moment of clarity that come up - five minutes before bed, in the middle of watching a movie, when you go to the balcony to get some fresh air for five minutes, when you're sitting on a chair at a party and you look around the room and time feels so slow, when you fantasize about your future, when you look back at some memory that you can't stop thinking about. It is in those moments, you realize not to every allow work and stress to occupy the mind, not for a single minute. It is in those same moments that you promise yourself to live fully, to make that call to invite someone for lunch you said you would, to strike up a conversation with someone you promised yourself you would. Only if this clarity lasted. Only if.
